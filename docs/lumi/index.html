Frequently Asked Questions

Here are the quickest answers to the questions most people ask first.

Do I need an account to browse Lumi Cinema?

No. Core browsing and discovery features are available without an account. Account sign-in is mainly used for syncing personal library features.

Why does Lumi ask for photo library access?

Photo library access is used only when you choose to save quote cards or other shared visual exports to your device.

How do I report a bug?

Send an email with your device type, app version, and a short description of the problem. Screenshots help a lot when available.
