Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 13, 2026

Lumi Cinema is designed to help users discover movies and shows, track personal viewing, browse quotes, and create shareable quote cards. This Privacy Policy explains what data may be used when you interact with the app.

Information We May Process

  • Account information when you choose to sign in, such as your email address.
  • Your app content, including watchlist items, favourites, watched history, notes, and lists.
  • Quote card and sharing actions when you choose to create or save media.
  • Your typed Recommend Tonight movie recommendation request and temporary query-refinement context if you choose to use AI recommendations.
  • Basic diagnostic information to investigate app crashes or bugs, when available.

How Information Is Used

  • To provide account-based features and sync your personal library data.
  • To improve recommendations, analytics, and app reliability.
  • To support features such as saving quote cards to your device.
  • To respond to support requests and product feedback.

Third-Party AI Services

Lumi offers an optional AI-powered feature called Recommend Tonight. Before this feature sends data to any third-party AI provider, the app asks for your permission in-app.

  • AI providers used for this feature: Google Gemini and OpenRouter.
  • Data sent for this feature: your typed movie recommendation request and temporary query-refinement context needed to generate recommendations.
  • Data not sent to those AI providers: your full library, your full account profile, and your sign-in credentials.
  • You can disable this permission later from the Account section in the app.

How AI Recommendation Data Is Used

  • To interpret your typed request in natural language.
  • To generate recommendation candidates and ranking guidance for the Recommend Tonight feature.
  • To refine results based on the context of the active recommendation request.

Photo Library Access

If you choose to save generated quote cards or similar media to your device, Lumi Cinema may request access to your photo library. This access is used only for the user-initiated save action.

Account Deletion

You can delete your account in the app from the Account section. If you need assistance, contact sodukle@gmail.com.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, contact sodukle@gmail.com.
