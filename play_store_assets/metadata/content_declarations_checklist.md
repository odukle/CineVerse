# Play Console Content Declarations Checklist

## App access
- If login is required for core app functionality, provide demo credentials or a guided video in Play Console.

## Ads
- Declare whether the app contains ads.

## Content rating
- Complete questionnaire accurately based on user-visible content.

## Target audience
- Select age groups.
- If not directed at children, ensure store listing and app behavior align.

## News
- Declare “No” unless app is a news app as defined by Play policy.

## Data safety
- Complete with accurate data collection/sharing/retention details.
- Match actual SDK and backend behavior.

## Permissions
- Review all Android permissions in merged manifest.
- Ensure each sensitive permission has feature justification.

## Region/legal
- Add required links and legal text for your target countries.
