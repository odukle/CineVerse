Lumi helps you decide what to watch faster, with less scrolling.

Discover movies and TV shows with rich filters, curated rails, and a cinematic browse experience. Use natural language to describe your mood, references, or constraints, and Lumi builds a recommendation plan to find strong matches.

Key features:
- AI-powered recommendation flow (natural-language prompts to search plan)
- “What should I watch tonight?” with semantic matching
- Hidden Gems discovery rail and genre exploration
- Trailers, cast/crew, awards, quotes, and full-plot support
- Watch provider links and deep-link resolution
- Personal library: watchlist, watched, favorites, notes, custom lists
- Watch history analytics with charts and shareable insights
- Episode tracker with reminders and notifications
- Beautiful modern UI, themed experience, and haptic interactions

Built for people who want better picks, faster:
- “Something like Interstellar, but less sci-fi”
- “A feel-good movie with great visuals tonight”
- “Underrated thrillers under 2 hours”

Important:
- Availability varies by region and provider.
- Some metadata (such as quotes/awards) may depend on third-party sources.
